Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

Royal Rush ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application and web game.

Summary: Royal Rush does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data is stored locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Royal Rush does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Financial information
  • Contact lists or social media data

Local Data Storage

Royal Rush stores game progress locally on your device using your browser's localStorage feature. This includes:

  • Game progress (upgrades, money, achievements)
  • Statistics (total draws, wins, etc.)
  • Daily bonus streak information
  • Prestige level and points

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

Royal Rush does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The game operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to play.

Children's Privacy

Royal Rush does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The game is suitable for all ages and contains no objectionable content.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your information being exposed through data breaches or unauthorized access to our systems.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@royalrush.game

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. However, you have full control over your local game data and can delete it at any time through your device settings.
